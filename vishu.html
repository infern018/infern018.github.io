CURRENT STATUS :

As of 29 April 2020, following are the major mishaps due to corona virus:

  • A 55-year-old individual from Hubei province in China may have been the first person to have contracted COVID-19, that case dates back to Nov. 17, 2019, according to the South Morning China Post.
  • There have been more than 3 million cases worldwide, with a mortality rate of 19%.
  • USA is the most affected country with approx 1 million cases, with 30% cases in New York itself.
  • Symptoms of COVID-19 may appear in as few as 2 days or as long as 14, and prevention is the only cure as no vaccine has been developed till time.
  • Older people, and people with pre-existing medical conditions (such as asthma, diabetes, heart disease) appear to be more vulnerable to becoming severely ill with the virus.
  • The IMF described the decline in economy due to COVID-19 as the worst since the Great Depression of the 1930s.

Where does India stand?

  • Currently there have been 30,000+ cases in the country with a mortality rate of 12%.
  • On the orders of the govt. India, a poulation of 1.37 billion is under lockdown since 33 days, which has controlled the contamination of the virus to a great extent.
  • Miseries of migrant labourers have worsen amid coronavirus pandemic and lockdown, as they have no transportation facilites and shelter to live.
  • Plasma therapy, is being used only for research and trial purposes, the health ministry warned.
  • India lost the tag of the fastest-growing major economy amid corona-virus as the predicted growth rate came down to 2.5%.
  • At least 7,696 people have recovered from the disease so far. Kerala witnessed one of the best recovery rates in the country.
